Who We Are

About Our Church

Welcome to GMSDA

God's Mandate Salvation and Dominion Assembly (GMSDA) is a vibrant Christian church rooted in Pentecostal/Charismatic faith and ministry. Located at Wali Estate (Believers Junction) along Rumoukwusi/Rumuodara Link Road in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.

Our Core Belief

We believe that every person can experience a transformed life through faith in Jesus Christ, and that believers are called to live victorious lives empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Led by Apostle Promise N. Baridon, our Founder and General Overseer, we emphasize both personal salvation and dominion — empowering believers to live victoriously over sin and challenges through Christ.

Charity & Community Support

God's Mandate Salvation and Dominion Assembly is propelled to carry out charity works as an expression of our faith. We help the poor and needy, support orphanages, and partner with government agencies to educate society against immoral activities.

Apostle Promise N. Baridon

Founder & General Overseer

Apostle Promise N. Baridon carries the divine mandate to lead the assembly in fulfilling God's purposes locally and beyond. Under his visionary leadership, GMSDA continues to experience remarkable growth and spiritual impact.
