Who We Are

About Our Church

Welcome to GMSDA

God's Mandate Salvation and Dominion Assembly (GMSDA) is a vibrant Christian church rooted in Pentecostal/Charismatic faith and ministry. Located at Wali Estate (Believers Junction) along Rumoukwusi/Rumuodara Link Road in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.

Our Core Belief

We believe that every person can experience a transformed life through faith in Jesus Christ, and that believers are called to live victorious lives empowered by the Holy Spirit. The church is a divine mandate — a calling to spread God's love and truth.

Led by Apostle Promise N. Baridon, our Founder and General Overseer, we emphasize both personal salvation (being born again in Christ) and dominion — meaning believers are empowered to live victoriously over sin, challenges, and the works of darkness through the authority of Jesus Christ.

✝️

Salvation in Christ

Salvation is the foundational Christian experience — turning away from sin and placing faith in Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior through repentance and receiving new life by the Holy Spirit.

👑

Dominion for Believers

The authority Jesus gives believers to overcome personal struggles, spiritual opposition, and to live a victorious Christian life in confidence and power.

🔄

Transformation

Building a community of believers who demonstrate God's truth and love in their homes, workplaces, and society through continuous spiritual growth.

Our Activities

What We Do

📖 Teaching & Preaching

We teach and preach the Word of God with clarity and power, helping believers understand and apply biblical principles to their daily lives.

🙏 Worship & Prayer

Heartfelt worship, prayer gatherings, and fellowship aimed at encountering God and equipping believers to grow spiritually.

🤝 Community Outreach

Leading people into a saving relationship with Christ and helping believers mature spiritually in their walk with God.

⚡ Spiritual Empowerment

Helping believers walk in their God-given authority and experience the transformative power of the Holy Spirit.

🤲

Charity & Community Support

God's Mandate Salvation and Dominion Assembly is propelled to carry out charity works as an expression of our faith. We are committed to helping the poor and needy, supporting orphanages, and partnering with government agencies to educate society against immoral activities.

We believe that true faith is demonstrated through love and service to humanity, especially to those in need. Our charity initiatives reflect our commitment to being the hands and feet of Jesus in our community.

Apostle Promise N. Baridon
